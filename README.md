## Project Title
Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (2014–2017)

## Business Objective
To identify revenue drivers, uncover profitability gaps, analyze the impact of discounts on profit, 
and understand customer behavior across segments and regions.

## Dataset Overview
The dataset analyzes 5,000+ transactional records from the Global Superstore dataset (2014-2017).
including revenue, profit, discount, customer segments, and regional performance and this dataset was gotten from Kaggle

## Tools Used
* Power BI
* DAX: DIVIDE, CALCULATE, SWITCH  
* Data Cleaning
- Set correct data types
- Remove Duplicates
- Remove Unwanted Columns
- Round Up numerical columns 
- added extra columns for analysis

## Key KPIs
1. EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW
- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Loss Orders (%)
- YoY Growth (Revenue & Profit)

2. PROFIT & DISCOUNT ANALYSIS
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Discount
- Loss Orders(%)

3. CUSTOMER & SEGMENT ANALYSIS
- Revenue
- Profit
- Unique Customers

 ## Key Insights 
OVERALL TREND (2014–2017)
- Revenue shows consistent growth across the years, with a peak in 2017
- while profit increased but at a slower rate than revenue and profit margin is not consistent, showing inefficiencies 
in cost of product or pricing strategy, (heavy and low discounting)

## Recommendations
- Reduce discount levels in low-performing categories
- Focus on products that have high-margin instead of just high sales volume
- work on pricing strategy in regions that are making loss
- Target profitable customer segments for growth
